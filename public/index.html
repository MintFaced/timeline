Questions & Answers

Who is the session for?

Anyone whose stories you want to keep. Parents, grandparents, aunts, uncles — anyone with a life worth remembering (which is everyone).

Where does the session take place?

We record the session at The Line art gallery in Hastings with tea and coffee served, a biscuit and a comfy chair to relax in.

What if they're shy or don't think they have stories?

Everyone says that at first. Our listeners are trained to gently draw out memories with warmth and patience. Within minutes, most people are sharing stories they haven't told in years.

How is the recording delivered?

You'll receive two USB keys, and a private link to download from at anytime in case the USB sticks are lost, or expired.

Can I give this as a gift?

Absolutely. It's one of the most meaningful gifts you can give. We can help you present it as a surprise — just let us know when you book.

What if they can't do a full hour?

That's completely fine. Some of the most beautiful recordings are 20 minutes long. We work at their pace, and there's never any pressure.

Is the recording shared publicly?

Never. Your recording is completely private. We don't publish, share, or use any recordings. They belong to your family alone.
