Questions & Answers

Who is a Legacy Session for?

Anyone whose values and wisdom you want to preserve. Parents, grandparents, aunts, uncles — anyone whose perspective and lessons deserve to be passed down (which is everyone).

Where does the session take place?

We hold Legacy Sessions at The Line art gallery in Hastings with tea and coffee served, a biscuit and a comfy chair to relax in.

What if they're not sure what to talk about?

That's completely normal. Our facilitators are trained to gently draw out what matters most — values, lessons, perspective — with warmth and patience. Within minutes, most people are sharing wisdom they've never put into words before.

How is the legacy keepsake delivered?

You'll receive two USB keys, and a private link to download from at anytime in case the USB sticks are lost, or expired.

Can I give this as a gift?

Absolutely. A Legacy Session is one of the most meaningful gifts you can give — especially for milestone birthdays or retirement. We can help you present it as a surprise.

What if they can't do a full hour?

That's completely fine. Some of the most powerful legacy conversations are 20 minutes long. We work at their pace, and there's never any pressure.

Is my family's legacy kept private?

Always. Your family's legacy is sacred to us. We never publish, share, or use anything from your session. It belongs to your family alone.
